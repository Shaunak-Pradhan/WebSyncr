  • Availability: Always Available Upon Prior Request 
  • Duration: 3 Hours
The All Through the Town Program includes:
  • Interact and feed camels and learn about the desert lifestyle! 
  • Ride a vintage bus around Old Dubai. 
  • Meet a Bedouin inside Bedouin tent.
  • Tour topics tailored to students' ages and curriculums. 
  • En Route Games on the bus for added excitement! 
  • Students leave with 3D Puzzle Gifts.

Meet the Storytellers

Ahmed

Ahmed shares his interesting memories and stories from before and after the union of the Emirates, and enjoys presenting his culture and religion to guests.

Khawla

Khawla is passionate about sharing Emirati culture through art, architecture, and heritage.

Yousef

Yousef is passionate on promoting his hometown, Ras AL Khaimah, emphasizing its rich history, culture and family-oriented environment.

Sara

Sara enjoys storytelling about the Emirati culture, heritage and history & she likes to share the similarities between her culture and other cultures.

Mohammed

Mohammed believes sharing cultural understanding and representing Emiratis is a huge responsibility.

Budoor
